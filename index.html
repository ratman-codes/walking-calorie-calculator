For grades −5% to +5%, calories follow the cubic equations ShapeSense derived from Margaria et al.'s "Energy Cost of Running" data (the same equations behind Omni's walking calculator); for +6% to +15%, the ACSM walking metabolic equation. Net burn subtracts the resting calories you'd have spent anyway during the walk — your TDEE ÷ 24 per hour if provided, otherwise a 1-MET resting rate. Gross numbers here should match Omni; the net number is the one your food log actually wants. The hands-on-desk discount trims only the activity portion (gross minus baseline), since supporting weight on a desk reduces the work of walking but can't reduce resting metabolism. Validated speed range: 0.62–4.66 mph (1–7.5 km/h).
